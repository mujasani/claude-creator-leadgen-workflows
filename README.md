# Claude Creator LeadGen Workflows

Open-source batch prompts for **Claude 4.6 (Opus & Sonnet)** that turn one prompt into 25 high-quality creator leads with digital product partnership ideas.

Built by **Sani Abdulmumini Sani** in Abuja, Nigeria while learning AI and helping indie creators monetize their audiences.

## What this repo contains
- Ready-to-copy batch prompt (the exact one I use daily)
- Full example output from a real batch (“No-Product Batch 3”)
- Strategic follow-up framework that actually closes deals

## The Batch Prompt (copy-paste into Claude)

Present 25 per batch in this table:  
No. | Handle | Name | Location | Niche | Followers | Email | What They Post | No-Product Signal | Digital Product Idea | Priority

I needed a repeatable way to find creators who have audience + engagement but no product yet. These prompts help me (and now you) generate warm leads + smart partnership ideas (90/10 splits, free samples, urgency lines, etc.) so indie makers can actually close their first clients.
